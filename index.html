Abstract

This project presents FJSP-LB-MK, a learning-based framework for flexible job shop scheduling under limited buffers and material kitting constraints. The proposed approach integrates graph-based representation learning with reinforcement learning to address complex industrial scheduling scenarios.

Video

Method Overview

Our framework models the scheduling problem as a graph and leverages attention-based neural networks to capture interactions between operations, machines, and buffers.

Results

Experimental results demonstrate superior performance compared with existing baselines on both synthetic benchmarks and real-world industrial datasets.
