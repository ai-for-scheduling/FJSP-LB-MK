A Deep Reinforcement Learning Method for Flexible Job Shop Scheduling under Limited Buffers and Material Kitting Constraints

Video

Description

This video demonstrates the simulation of a production line for the FJSP-LB-MK problem, implemented in Siemens Plant Simulation. Steel plates (jobs) are first released from the loading storage and dispatched to parallel machines via the loading queue. After plate transfer and cutting, the resulting parts are conveyed to the sorting stage, where robotic stations with limited pallets perform part sorting. The simulation provides an intuitive visualization of the scheduling process and a comparison of the scheduling behaviors of different methods.
